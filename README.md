📊 Screentime vs Mental Wellness Analysis
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the relationship between daily screen time and mental wellness using Microsoft Excel. With the increasing use of smartphones, laptops, and digital devices, people spend a large portion of their day in front of screens. This study aims to understand how increased screen time impacts mental health factors such as stress levels, sleep quality, focus, and overall well-being.

The project uses a dataset containing information about individuals' daily screen usage and mental wellness indicators. By analyzing the data with Excel tools, we identify patterns and insights that highlight how digital device usage may influence mental health.

🎯 Project Objectives

Analyze the relationship between screen time and mental wellness

Identify how excessive screen usage affects stress levels

Understand the impact of screen time on sleep quality

Evaluate the effect of digital device usage on focus and productivity

Provide data-driven insights that may help improve digital well-being habits

📂 Dataset Description

The dataset includes the following variables:

Age – Age of the participant

Daily Screen Time (hours) – Average screen usage per day

Sleep Quality – Rating of sleep quality

Stress Level – Self-reported stress level

Focus Level – Ability to concentrate during daily tasks

Mental Wellness Score – Overall mental health indicator

📈 Analysis Performed

The following analysis was conducted using Excel:

Data cleaning and formatting

Screen time distribution analysis

Relationship between screen time and stress levels

Impact of screen time on sleep quality

Correlation between screen time and focus levels

Creation of Pivot Tables and charts for visualization

📊 Key Insights

Higher screen time is often associated with increased stress levels.

Excessive device usage may negatively affect sleep quality.

Individuals with moderate screen time tend to show better focus and mental wellness scores.

Balanced screen usage may support better digital well-being.
